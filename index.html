Welcome To Crowded!

We're a small (but growing!) community of geeks who just wanted a digital place to hang out with other geeks and talk about what we love.

Here you will find people with diverse interests such as:
Video Games
There is a lot of Destiny 2 talk going on, but there are also people playing Fortnite, PUBG, and many other games.
Programming
Whether you want to throw some ideas off someone else, discuss something you found interesting, or simply join in on the banter of the Ruby vs PHP debate we collectively have over 20 years of programming experience (but we won't do your homework for you!)
Space
We're fans of the final frontier! We reguraly watch live streams of SpaceX launches and even on occasion get together to watch past launches together on YouTube.
TV
We're living in the second golden age of television and we're here to talk about it all! We have fans of science fiction, Game of Thrones, Rick and Morty and more! If you don't find a channel, tell an admin and we'll get it created for you!
Rage
Are you angry about something? Feel free to shout it into the void with our very own rage channel! It's very theraputic!

What are you waiting for? Join us on Discord today!
