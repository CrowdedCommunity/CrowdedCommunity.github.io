The Crowded Group is a community and without people we wouldn't be where we are today. As the saying goes, many hands make light work, and we need many people to help keep our community vibrant and a place people want to return. Our goal is not to make everyone happy, but to make it a place where they feel like their presence is meaningful. If you're interested in helping in that way, take a look at the roles and responsibilities we have below and reach out to the admins if you feel like you might be a good fit.

Admins

Admins play a crucial role in the day to day operations of our community. From managing Discord, setting up new tools for us to use, activity on social media, they have their hands in a little bit of everything. This role will require a certain level of tech-savyness.

Requirements

  • Active member of the community
  • Demonstrates an earned trust of the community
  • Nominated and selected by unanimous vote of the current admins

Responsibilities

  • Manage the Discord server
    • Set up custom commands for the community to use
    • Assign non-rank based roles to the appropriate people
  • Responsible for giving people the right access to community platforms
  • Publish website updates
  • Determine community policies

Community Manager

Requirements

  • Active member of the community
  • Demonstrates an earned trust of the community
  • Reached rank of Recruiter in Discord

Responsibilities

  • Managing social networks and active publishing on all channels
    • Facebook
    • Twitter/Mastadon
    • Discord
  • Promoting scheduled events, such as giveaways within our community or relevant events happening that our community should be interested in
  • Actively seeks out ways to draw in new members

Community Moderator

Requirements

  • Active member of the community
  • Demonstrates an earned trust of the community
  • Reached rank of Member in Discord

Responsibilities

  • Responsible for making sure the community adheres to any written or unwritten policies formed by the admins
  • Answer questions or direct people to the appropriate sources of information for our community
  • Learn and utilize our custom commands to help the community
  • Suggest custom commands that will help the community

Advocate

Requirements

  • Active member of the community
  • Reached a rank of Initiate in Discord or demonstrates an earned trust in the community

Responsibilities

  • Actively seek out and post about new information pertaining to the game they choose to be an advocate for
  • Promote and schedule events for the game
    • Events would be available to the community or public

Clan Admin

Currently, only for Destiny, but will include other games as our community grows. We are already planning on starting one for Anthem when it's available.

Requirements

  • Active member of the community
  • Reached a rank of Member in Discord or demonstrates an earned trust in the community
  • Actively plays with clan members
  • Actively posts games on the Crowded Gaming group

Responsibilities

  • Promote and recruit for clan to incoming players
  • Post games on the Crowded Gaming group
  • Help other clanmates through quests, story, and endgame content
